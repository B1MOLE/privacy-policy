Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

The Seed app is designed to work offline. We do not collect or store your personal vocabulary data on our own servers.

2. AI Processing (Google Gemini)

To categorize your vocabulary words, this app uses the Google Gemini API. When you stash a new word, that specific text is sent to Google's servers for processing. No personal identifiers (like your name or email) are sent with the word.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the Pixabay API to provide images for level nodes. No user data is shared with Pixabay.

4. Contact

If you have questions, contact us via the support link in the app.
