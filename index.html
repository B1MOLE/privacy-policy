Privacy Policy for Seed

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application "Seed" (the "App"), provided by Alexey G ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App.

Contact: alexeyg2006@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information. When you create an account or sign in, we collect information such as your email address, nickname, authentication provider, and Firebase user ID. If you use email/password sign-in, your credentials are processed by Firebase Authentication on our behalf.
  • Profile and settings. We store settings such as your interface language, learning language, daily goal, theme preference, reminder time, and related profile preferences.
  • Learning and progress data. We store data needed to run the App, such as your words, topics, review history, streaks, achievements, quests, in-app currency, premium status, and similar progress data.
  • Imported or pasted content. If you paste text or choose files to import (such as .txt, .csv, .doc, or .docx), the App reads that content so it can extract words, build learning lists, or generate study content from it.
  • AI and translation requests. When you use AI-generated features or translation features, the App sends the text you choose to process (for example words, phrases, topic names, or imported snippets) together with language settings. We do not intentionally include profile fields such as your email address or nickname in those content prompts.
  • Microphone input. If you use speaking practice, the App accesses your microphone while that feature is active. Seed does not store raw audio recordings or upload raw microphone recordings to our own servers. Speech recognition may be handled by your device or platform speech-recognition provider under that provider's own terms.
  • Support requests. If you contact us, we collect the information you include in your message.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and app information. We may collect device model, OS version, app version, locale, timezone, and connectivity-related information needed to run the App and keep data in sync.
  • Crash and diagnostics data. Firebase Crashlytics collects crash reports and related diagnostics such as stack traces, device model, OS version, and app version.
  • Notification data. If you enable reminders or notifications, we may store your Firebase Cloud Messaging token, timezone, and reminder settings so we can send reminder notifications.
  • Advertising and consent data. If ads are shown, Google AdMob and Google's consent tools may process your advertising ID, consent choices, and ad interaction data. Google may also infer approximate location from IP address for ads, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
  • Purchase data. If you make purchases or subscriptions, RevenueCat and Google Play may process your app user ID, product identifiers, entitlement status, purchase token, and transaction status. We do not receive your full payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the App and keep you signed in.
  • To sync your profile, progress, and settings across devices.
  • To personalize your learning experience.
  • To translate text and generate study content you request.
  • To process imported content you choose to use in the App.
  • To send optional push reminders and notifications if you enable them.
  • To show ads according to your consent choices where required.
  • To process purchases, subscriptions, and premium access.
  • To diagnose crashes, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • To respond to support, privacy, and account-deletion requests.

4. Permissions the App Requests

  • Internet - to connect to Firebase, translation services, image services, ads, and purchase services.
  • Access network state - to adapt behavior based on connectivity.
  • Post notifications - to send reminders and other notifications. You can deny this and still use the core App.
  • Record audio - only when you use the speaking practice feature.
  • Billing - to support purchases and subscriptions.

The Android build may also include supporting capabilities used by notification, messaging, and ads SDKs, such as vibration, wake locks, cloud-messaging delivery, and ad ID access. Seed does not request precise location permission.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Cloud data is stored in Firebase services for as long as your account remains active or as otherwise needed to provide the App.
  • On-device data is stored on your device using tools such as ObjectBox, SharedPreferences, and image caches so the App can work faster and support offline use where available.
  • Imported and generated content may be stored locally or in your account data when needed to provide the feature you used.
  • Third-party processing of text sent to AI, translation, image, ads, purchase, and other providers is handled under those providers' own retention practices and terms.
  • Deletion. If you delete your account, we aim to delete associated server-side account data within 48 hours unless a longer retention period is required by law or needed for fraud prevention, dispute handling, or legal compliance.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as described in this policy:

  • With the third-party providers listed in Section 2, as needed to operate the App.
  • With law enforcement or government authorities, if legally required.
  • To protect the rights, safety, security, and integrity of Seed, our users, or others.
  • In the event of a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, and to withdraw consent for optional processing.

  • Manage notifications - you can change notification permission in your device settings.
  • Manage ad settings - you can reset your advertising ID and manage ad-related settings on your device.
  • Delete your account in-app - use Settings -> Delete Account.
  • Contact us - email alexeyg2006@gmail.com to request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data.

We aim to respond to privacy requests within 7 business days.

8. Children's Privacy

Seed is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us and we will work to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. When required, we rely on the safeguards offered by those providers under their applicable terms.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted connections: HTTPS/TLS for network traffic.
  • Provider-managed security features such as Firebase security controls and encryption at rest.
  • Access controls intended to limit data access to the correct user account.
  • Firebase App Check and related integrity checks to reduce abuse of our backend services.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
