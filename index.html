Privacy Policy for Seed

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Seed handles your information. By using the app, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Data Collection & Authentication

Seed uses Firebase Authentication (Google OAuth) to manage user accounts. When you sign in, we collect your email address and a unique user ID to sync your vocabulary progress across devices.

Your vocabulary maps are stored in a secure database provided by Firebase. We do not sell this data to third parties.

2. AI Processing (Google Gemini)

To categorize and organize your vocabulary, Seed uses the Google Gemini API. When you add a word, that specific text is sent to Google's servers for processing. This data is sent without personal identifiers (like your email or name).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Pixabay API: Used to provide images for level nodes. No user data is shared with Pixabay.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process "Gem" purchases. We do not see or store your credit card information; it is handled entirely by Google.

4. Account Deletion & Data Retention

Google Play requires that users have a clear way to delete their accounts. If you wish to delete your Seed account and all associated data (including your vocabulary maps and email record), you can do so by:

  • In-App: Going to Settings > Delete Account.
  • Via Email: Sending a request to [alexeyg2006@gmail.com] with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Once requested, your account and all associated vocabulary data will be permanently deleted from our Firebase servers within 48 hours.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please contact Aleksei Goncharov at [alexeyg2006@gmail.com].
